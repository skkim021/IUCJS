International Universtiy Connection provides a place for students from all over the world to gain access to prestiges universities in the United States. Students will be able to gain access to information for hundreds of universities.

About Us

Our business concept is to help international students, who want to study in U.S., to find best match schools with their levels (TOEFL/IELTS/GMAT/GRE/GPA/Working experience) by our website, instead of using agency. Also, our website supports U.S. school to reach international students easily and effectively by sharing students information.
